Sonoma County is a beautiful and relaxing place to spend a weekend, but August is peak season so please be aware that rooms book quickly. After looking at all of the options, we reserved a block of rooms at both the Flamingo Resort and Spa and Hotel la Rose in Santa Rosa. When you call, tell them that you are booking for the Kittner Tucker wedding.

The Flamingo has a nice pool which is the perfect place to relax and catch up with friends and family. Hotel la Rose has a quaint historic vibe and is within walking distance of some shops and restaurants. Santa Rosa is about a 20 minute drive from Beltane Ranch. We will provide transportation from both hotels to the wedding, but if you don't like the idea of being beholden to a bus schedule, Uber and Lyft run in the area and there are also a number of local taxi services.
