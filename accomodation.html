Sonoma County is beautiful and relaxing place to spend a weekend, but August is peak season so please be aware that rooms book quickly. After looking at all of the options, we reserved a block of rooms at both the Flamingo Resort and Spa and Hotel La Rose in Santa Rosa. The Flamingo has a nice pool which is the perfect place to relax and catch up with friends and family. Hotel la Rose has a quaint historic vibe and within walking distance of some shops and restaurants. Santa Rosa is about a 20 minute drive from Beltane Ranch. We will provide transportation from both hotels to the wedding, but if you don't like the idea of being beholden to a bus schedule, Uber and Lyft run in the area and there are also a number of local taxi services.

Flamingo Resort and Spa
www.flamingoresort.com
2777 4th St.
Santa Rosa, CA 95405

If you are looking for a more up scale experience, The Olea is a lovely boutique hotel in Glen Ellen, and only a couple of miles from Beltane Ranch.

The Olea Hotel
www.oleahotel.com
5131 Warm Springs Rd.
Glen Ellen, CA 95442

If you would like to stay in the heart of downtown Sonoma without spending a fortune, The Sonoma Hotel is a small Inn right on the square. The rooms are quaint but on the smaller side. Be aware that the hotel does not have an elevator and all rooms are on the upper floors. The Sonoma Hotel is about 7 miles from Beltane Ranch. We have rooms reserved there, but they will release them 90 days before the wedding date.

The Sonoma Hotel
www.sonomahotel.com
110 W Spain St.
Sonoma, CA 95476

Airbnb and VRBO are both great options to find an interesting alternative to a standard hotel.
