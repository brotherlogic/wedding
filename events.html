You can fly into Oakland or San Francisco International airports, and the wedding venue is about a 1 hour 30 minute drive from either. There are also flights to Santa Rosa airport, though availability may be limited. Be aware that traffic can add 1 hour to your journey time if you are travelling during commute hours. Click on the accommodations tab for information about hotels. We've reserved rooms at two Santa Rosa Hotels, brunch will be at the Flamingo Hotel.

A note about weather for those of you not familiar with the area. In August, it will be hot during the day, but temperatures could drop once the sun goes down. Both the welcome dinner and wedding are outdoors, so bring an extra layer.
